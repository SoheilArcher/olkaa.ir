# Deprecation Policy

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Initial policy

This policy defines how products, modules, features, or APIs are deprecated.

## Deprecation Criteria

- Product or feature is no longer strategically aligned.
- Replacement exists or is planned.
- Security or maintenance risk is too high.
- Usage is low and support cost is high.
- Architecture no longer supports safe operation.

## Rules

- Do not remove active functionality without a documented decision.
- Provide migration guidance where applicable.
- Preserve historical documentation.
- Update product status to Deprecated before Archived.
