# Product Lifecycle Management

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Initial system

Every company product must be managed through the Product Lifecycle Management system.

The PLM system defines how Fava Imen Olka products move from idea to research, prototype, MVP, development, testing, production, maintenance, deprecation, and archival.

## Lifecycle Stages

- Idea
- Research
- Prototype
- MVP
- Development
- Testing
- Production
- Maintenance
- Deprecated
- Archived

## Required Product Records

Every product should have:

- Current lifecycle stage.
- Product owner.
- Product status.
- Roadmap alignment.
- Feature registry.
- Release plan.
- Version history.
- Technical debt register.
- Bug classification rules.
- Customer feedback records.
- Product health score.

## PLM Rules

- Do not mark a product as Production unless delivery, testing, documentation, support, and security readiness are verified.
- Do not add features to a release without approval and roadmap alignment.
- Customer feedback must be recorded without exposing private or sensitive information.
- Technical debt must be visible and reviewed regularly.
- Deprecated products must have migration, communication, and archival plans.
