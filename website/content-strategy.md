# Website Content Strategy

Purpose: Define how olkaa.ir should communicate Fava Imen Olka's company identity, capabilities, products, services, knowledge, and research.

## Content Principles

- Use professional English.
- Keep Persian company name where useful: فاوا ایمن الکا.
- Do not overclaim.
- Clearly separate completed experience, in-progress products, research, and planned products.
- Keep product status visible.
- Prefer precise, factual wording over marketing exaggeration.
- Reuse the Enterprise Manual as the source of truth.

## Audience

- Airport and aviation stakeholders.
- Enterprise customers.
- Infrastructure and network customers.
- Hospitality and tourism organizations.
- Government and public-sector buyers.
- Technical partners.
- Job candidates.

## Message Pillars

- Enterprise software and operational systems.
- Airport systems and passenger-service workflows.
- Infrastructure, network, and cybersecurity capability.
- AI-enabled future product strategy.
- Documentation, standards, and long-term maintainability.

## Content Governance

- Product pages must be aligned with `products/`.
- Public SEO service pages are tracked in `website/service-pages.md`.
- Roadmap language must be aligned with `roadmap/`.
- Research pages must be aligned with `research/`.
- Knowledge Center content must be reviewed for security before publication.
- Client references must follow approval rules in `company/clients.md`.

## Editorial Workflow

- Draft content in the Enterprise Manual.
- Review for accuracy and overclaiming.
- Confirm product status.
- Confirm security and privacy safety.
- Publish only approved wording.
- Update documentation when website content changes.
