# Industries Page Content Architecture

Purpose: Explain the industries Fava Imen Olka serves or targets, while avoiding unverified claims about active relationships.

## Airports

Airport systems, passenger-service workflows, CIP/VIP coordination, FIDS, RMS, check-in software, reporting, and operational infrastructure.

## Airlines

Passenger service coordination, reservation-related workflows, operational support, reporting, and integration opportunities where approved.

## Hotels

Hotel reservation systems, hospitality workflows, connectivity, infrastructure, support, and reporting.

## Government

Enterprise systems, secure infrastructure, documentation, reporting, access control, and operational support for public-sector environments.

## Enterprise

ERP, identity, mail, network logging, cybersecurity, server operations, dashboards, and long-term engineering documentation.

## Healthcare

Planned and potential area for secure infrastructure, access control, reporting, operational software, and AI-assisted workflows, subject to compliance review.

## Education

Systems for examination workflows, internship management, identity, reporting, infrastructure, and secure operations.

## Tourism

Tourism agencies, hospitality services, passenger services, reservation workflows, reporting, and infrastructure support.
