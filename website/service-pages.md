# Public SEO Service Pages

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Active website content

This document defines the approved public service pages used by `olkaa.ir` for search visibility and factual service discovery.

## Purpose

The service pages help search engines and customers understand Fava Imen Olka's public service areas without overclaiming product maturity.

## Published Pages

- `/services/aviation-software/` — Aviation software and airport operations systems.
- `/services/datacenter/` — Datacenter, network, monitoring, and infrastructure services.
- `/services/ipv4-leasing/` — IPv4 leasing and IP management.
- `/services/ai-call-center/` — Planned AI-assisted call center and customer-service workflow.
- `/services/home-check-in/` — Home Check-in passenger-service workflow.
- `/services/cip-express/` — CIP Express airport hospitality workflow.

## Content Rules

- Pages must use factual language.
- Product or service status must be visible where relevant.
- Pages must link back to the official company identity and contact path.
- Sitemap must include every published public service page.
- Admin, portal, and internal staff pages must remain excluded from search indexing.
