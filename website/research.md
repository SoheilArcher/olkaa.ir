# Research Page Content Architecture

Purpose: Present research areas without implying that research topics are completed products or approved commercial commitments.

## Research Areas

- AI
- Cybersecurity
- Networking
- Aviation
- Enterprise software
- Market analysis

## Content Types

- Research notes.
- Technology evaluations.
- Market analysis.
- Product discovery.
- Security research.
- Architecture experiments.
- AI evaluation reports.

## Research Page Sections

- Featured Research
- AI Research
- Cybersecurity Research
- Aviation Research
- Enterprise Software Research
- Market Analysis
- Open Questions

## Editorial Rules

- Separate findings from recommendations.
- Include sources where possible.
- Clearly mark assumptions.
- Do not present early research as production capability.
- Link research to product roadmap only when approved.
