# Home Page Content Architecture

Purpose: Present Fava Imen Olka as an enterprise technology company with documented capability in airport systems, infrastructure services, software development, and emerging AI-enabled products.

## Hero

- Primary message: Fava Imen Olka builds enterprise software, airport systems, infrastructure services, and AI-enabled products for organizations that need reliable operations.
- Supporting message: The company combines documented experience in aviation, hospitality, network operations, and enterprise systems with a disciplined product roadmap.
- Primary call to action: Explore Products
- Secondary call to action: Contact Us

## Company Introduction

Introduce Fava Imen Olka / فاوا ایمن الکا as a registered technology company with offices in Tehran / Mirdamad, Kish Island, and Imam Khomeini Airport City.

Content should mention:

- Software development.
- Airport systems.
- Network and infrastructure services.
- Cybersecurity and server operations.
- Current and future AI-enabled product lines.
