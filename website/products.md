# Products Page Content Architecture

Purpose: Present the product portfolio with clear product status and careful separation between current development, research, and planned products.

## Product Status Legend

- Production: Delivered, verified, and supported.
- In Development: Actively being defined or built.
- Research: Being investigated and evaluated.
- Planned: Intended product direction, not yet delivered.

## Products

## Airport ERP

Status: In Development

Enterprise platform for airport operations, passenger-service workflows, CIP/VIP coordination, finance, reporting, audit logs, and airport-specific integrations.

## Market AI

Status: Research

AI-assisted market analysis product line for research, signal interpretation, report generation, and decision support with governed data sources.

## VPN Accounting

Status: Planned

Billing, subscription, customer management, payment tracking, renewal, and access-status platform for VPN and connectivity services.

## Mail Server

Status: Planned

Managed mail server product for organizational email, domain administration, mail delivery, security, backup, monitoring, and support.

## NetLog

Status: Planned

Network logging and audit platform for collecting, retaining, searching, reporting, and analyzing network activity and operational events.

## URL Reporting

Status: Planned

URL activity reporting product for web access visibility, categorization, compliance review, dashboards, and audit exports.

## Active Directory

Status: Planned

Identity and directory-service product line for centralized user management, groups, policies, access reviews, authentication integration, and audit logs.

## AI Call Center

Status: Planned

AI-assisted call center product for conversation summaries, routing, knowledge retrieval, customer context, quality review, and human handoff.
