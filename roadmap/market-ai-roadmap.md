# Vision

Build a research-driven AI product that helps users analyze markets, interpret signals, generate structured reports, and make better decisions with transparent assumptions and governed data sources.

# Current Stage

Market AI is in Research. The product needs approved use cases, data-source rules, evaluation criteria, and risk controls before it can move toward development.

# Version History

- v1.0.0: Initial roadmap created.

# v1.0

- Define target users and research questions.
- Identify approved and prohibited data sources.
- Document assumptions, limitations, and risk controls.
- Create evaluation criteria for usefulness, accuracy, explainability, and safety.
- Prototype research workflows without production claims.

# v1.1

- Build repeatable report templates.
- Test structured market summaries with controlled datasets.
- Add source tracking and confidence notes.
- Define review workflow for AI-generated analysis.
- Document lessons learned from experiments.

# v2.0

- Move selected validated workflows into product development if approved.
- Add dashboards, watchlists, alerts, and exportable reports.
- Add governed user access and audit logging.
- Integrate with the engineering knowledge base and research folder where appropriate.

# Future Ideas

- AI-generated market briefs.
- Scenario comparison and opportunity scoring.
- Natural-language queries over approved datasets.
- Automated research updates with source references.
- Integration with trading or market-analysis workflows where legally and operationally approved.

# Risks

- Poor data quality or unclear source rights.
- AI outputs may be inaccurate or overconfident.
- Users may treat research output as financial advice without proper controls.
- Regulatory, privacy, or licensing constraints may limit data use.
- Product claims may exceed research maturity.

# Dependencies

- Approved data-source inventory.
- AI governance and evaluation standards.
- Security and access-control requirements.
- Research review process.
- Product owner and domain expert review.

# Success Metrics

- Research questions and sources are documented.
- Every prototype has evaluation notes.
- AI outputs include source and limitation handling.
- No production claim is made before approval.
- Validated workflows are promoted through documented decisions.
