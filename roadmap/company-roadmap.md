# Vision

Build Fava Imen Olka into a disciplined enterprise technology company with documented knowledge, clear product lines, reliable operations, and specialized strength in airport systems, infrastructure services, AI-enabled workflows, and enterprise software.

# Current Stage

The Enterprise Manual, engineering knowledge base, product catalog, and organizational memory folders are being established as the single source of truth. Product lines are documented, but most are not yet documented as production products in this manual.

# Risks

- Overclaiming product maturity before implementation is verified.
- Fragmented documentation if teams do not update the manual.
- Product scope expanding faster than engineering capacity.
- Security, privacy, or compliance requirements being discovered too late.
- Unclear ownership of product decisions and operational procedures.

# Dependencies

- Product owners and document owners.
- Architecture, security, and design-system standards.
- Verified client and project references.
- Engineering capacity for MVP delivery.
- Approved data sources and AI governance.
- Deployment, monitoring, and support processes.

# Success Metrics

- All major company decisions and product changes are documented.
- Product pages show current status without overclaiming.
- Roadmaps are reviewed and updated at least quarterly.
- New production issues generate lessons learned when appropriate.
- Product MVPs have documented scope, risks, dependencies, and acceptance criteria before implementation.
