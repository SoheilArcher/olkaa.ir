# Enterprise Finance

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Initial architecture

This document tracks high-level financial indicators for management review.

## Financial Indicators

- Revenue.
- Recurring revenue.
- Outstanding invoices.
- Cost categories.
- Product or service profitability.
- Contract value.
- Renewal risk.
- Cash-flow notes.

## Rules

- Do not record unsupported financial claims.
- Keep sensitive finance details restricted.
- Link financial indicators to contracts and executive reports where appropriate.
