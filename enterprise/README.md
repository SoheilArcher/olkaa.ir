# Enterprise Operating System

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Initial architecture

The Enterprise Operating System is the internal operating system of Fava Imen Olka.

This is NOT an ERP.

It is the executive layer above all products. It is the single source of truth for company management.

Every product, every project, every strategic decision, every roadmap, and every executive report must eventually be represented here.

## Purpose

The Enterprise Operating System tracks:

- Products
- Projects
- Customers
- Contracts
- Employees
- Company KPIs
- Strategic Roadmap
- Company Decisions
- Risks
- Financial Indicators
- Executive Reports

## Operating Rules

- Do not treat this folder as application code.
- Keep management records factual and current.
- Link company-level records to product, roadmap, finance, customer, and decision documents where useful.
- Do not overclaim product status, revenue, customer activity, or delivery progress.
- Preserve historical executive knowledge through dated updates rather than deleting context.
