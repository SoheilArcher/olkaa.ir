# Executive Dashboard

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Initial architecture

The executive dashboard is the management view of Fava Imen Olka. It should show company health, product progress, project status, financial indicators, documentation maturity, infrastructure status, and strategic risks.

## Revenue

Purpose: Track high-level revenue indicators.

Suggested fields:

- Monthly revenue.
- Quarterly revenue.
- Revenue by product or service line.
- Recurring revenue.
- Outstanding invoices.
- Revenue risk notes.

## Active Products

Purpose: Show current product portfolio status.

Suggested fields:

- Product name.
- Product status.
- Owner.
- Current milestone.
- Health indicator.
- Next decision needed.

## Active Projects

Purpose: Track delivery and internal execution.

Suggested fields:

- Project name.
- Related product.
- Owner.
- Customer or internal sponsor.
- Current status.
- Timeline risk.
- Next action.

## Active Customers

Purpose: Show current customer and relationship status.

Suggested fields:

- Customer name.
- Sector.
- Related product or service.
- Relationship status.
- Open risks.
- Account owner.

## Monthly Goals

Purpose: Track executive priorities for the current month.

Suggested fields:

- Goal.
- Owner.
- Status.
- Dependency.
- Completion evidence.

## Product Health

Purpose: Track product maturity and operational confidence.

Suggested fields:

- Product status.
- Documentation status.
- Roadmap status.
- Security review status.
- Support readiness.
- Technical risk.

## Roadmap Progress

Purpose: Track company and product roadmap execution.

Suggested fields:

- Roadmap item.
- Target period.
- Owner.
- Status.
- Blockers.
- Decision required.

## Technical Debt

Purpose: Track engineering debt that affects reliability, security, velocity, or maintainability.

Suggested fields:

- Debt item.
- Related system.
- Severity.
- Owner.
- Mitigation plan.
- Target review date.

## Documentation Coverage

Purpose: Track whether products, processes, decisions, and lessons are documented.

Suggested fields:

- Product documentation coverage.
- Architecture documentation coverage.
- Security documentation coverage.
- Lessons learned coverage.
- ADR coverage.
- Knowledge-base updates.

## AI Usage

Purpose: Track approved AI usage across products and operations.

Suggested fields:

- AI use case.
- Related product.
- Approval status.
- Data sensitivity.
- Evaluation status.
- Risk notes.

## Infrastructure Status

Purpose: Track high-level infrastructure health.

Suggested fields:

- Critical services.
- Server status.
- Network status.
- Backup status.
- Security alerts.
- Open incidents.
