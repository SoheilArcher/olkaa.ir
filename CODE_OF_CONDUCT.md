# Code Of Conduct

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Initial governance

Fava Imen Olka expects professional, respectful, and constructive collaboration across all repositories and documentation spaces.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate respectfully.
- Focus feedback on work, not individuals.
- Be precise, factual, and constructive.
- Respect confidentiality, customer information, and company knowledge.
- Help maintain a professional engineering culture.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sharing private, sensitive, or confidential information without permission.
- Publishing secrets, credentials, customer data, or production data.
- Misrepresenting product status, company capability, or customer relationships.
- Disruptive or intentionally misleading contributions.

## Review And Enforcement

Maintainers may request changes, close issues, reject pull requests, remove inappropriate content, or escalate concerns according to company policy.

## Reporting

Report concerns to the repository owner or approved internal contact. Sensitive reports should be handled privately.
