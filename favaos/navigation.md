# FavaOS Navigation

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Navigation design

## Navigation Model

FavaOS uses a left sidebar with grouped navigation. The sidebar should remain visible on desktop and collapse on smaller screens.

## Left Sidebar

## Overview

- Executive Dashboard
- Executive Reports
- Company KPIs

## Management

- Products
- Projects
- Customers
- Contracts
- Finance
- HR

## Strategy

- Roadmaps
- Decisions
- ADR
- Risks
- Research

## Knowledge

- Knowledge Base
- Documentation
- Standards
- Lessons Learned

## Operations

- Infrastructure
- AI Center
- Meetings
- Analytics

## Administration

- Users
- Roles
- Permissions
- Audit Log
- Settings

## Navigation Rules

- Show only modules available to the user's role.
- Keep current module and current record visible.
- Use status badges for products, projects, risks, and roadmap items.
- Provide quick links from records to related Enterprise Manual documents.
