# FavaOS Dashboard

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Widget design only

This document describes dashboard widgets only. It does not define implementation.

## Executive Summary

Shows a concise leadership summary for the current review period.

Widgets:

- Company health.
- Highest priority risks.
- Decisions needed.
- Roadmap progress.
- Monthly goals.

## Products

Widgets:

- Active products.
- Product status by category.
- Products by owner.
- Next milestones.
- Product health.

## Projects

Widgets:

- Active projects.
- Blocked projects.
- Overdue milestones.
- Projects by owner.
- Project risk summary.

## Customers

Widgets:

- Active customers.
- Customers by sector.
- Customer relationship status.
- Open customer risks.
- Public reference approval status.

## Contracts

Widgets:

- Active contracts.
- Upcoming renewals.
- Contract risks.
- Outstanding obligations.
- Contracts by customer.

## Finance

Widgets:

- Monthly revenue indicator.
- Outstanding invoice indicator.
- Revenue by product or service line.
- Contract value indicator.
- Financial risk summary.

## HR

Widgets:

- Team capacity.
- Workload indicators.
- Open role needs.

- Employee responsibility map.

## Documentation
