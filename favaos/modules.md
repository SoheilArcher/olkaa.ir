# FavaOS Modules

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Architecture design

## Executive Dashboard

Provides leadership visibility into products, projects, revenue indicators, customers, roadmap progress, risks, technical debt, AI usage, and infrastructure status.

## Product Management

Tracks product portfolio, product status, owners, versions, roadmaps, milestones, risks, documentation, and next decisions.

## Project Management

Tracks internal and customer-facing projects, owners, status, timelines, dependencies, blockers, and delivery risks.

## Customer Management

Tracks customer records, sectors, relationship status, account owners, projects, contracts, and public reference approval status.

## Contracts

Tracks contract records, obligations, renewals, financial indicators, risks, and related customers or products.

## Finance

Tracks executive-level financial indicators, revenue notes, outstanding invoices, contract values, and financial risks.

## HR

Tracks management-level employee records, teams, roles, responsibilities, workload indicators, and skill coverage.

## Knowledge Base

Tracks knowledge entries from solved problems, architecture decisions, deployment guides, troubleshooting procedures, lessons learned, and best practices.

## Documentation

Tracks Enterprise Manual documents, versions, owners, review dates, and documentation coverage.

## Roadmaps

Tracks product and company roadmap items, target periods, status, dependencies, and success metrics.

## Decisions

Tracks important business and engineering decisions with context, alternatives, final decisions, consequences, date, and status.

## ADR

Tracks architecture decision records and links them to products, systems, and implementation risks.

## Risks

Tracks business, technical, operational, financial, security, infrastructure, and documentation risks.

## Research

Tracks AI, cybersecurity, networking, aviation, enterprise software, market analysis, and automation research.

## Infrastructure

Tracks servers, services, network dependencies, backups, monitoring, incidents, and operational status.

## AI Center

Tracks AI use cases, approvals, data sensitivity, evaluation status, prompts, model choices, risks, and product connections.
