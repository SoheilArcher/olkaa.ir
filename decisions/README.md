# Decisions

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Initial structure

This folder stores important business and engineering decisions for Fava Imen Olka.

## Naming

Use sequential decision records:

- `DEC-0001.md`
- `DEC-0002.md`

## Template

```markdown
# DEC-0001: Decision Title

## Decision

State the decision in one or two clear sentences.

## Context

Explain the business, technical, operational, or strategic context.

## Alternatives

List the alternatives that were considered.

## Final Decision

Document the selected option and why it was chosen.

## Consequences

Describe benefits, tradeoffs, risks, costs, ownership, and follow-up work.

## Date

YYYY-MM-DD

## Status

Proposed | Accepted | Superseded | Deprecated
```

## Rules

- Record decisions when they affect strategy, products, architecture, operations, security, sales, or delivery.
- Do not delete historical decisions.
- If a decision changes, mark the old record as superseded and link to the new record.
