## Recommended Structure

```markdown
# Research Title

Date: YYYY-MM-DD  
Author: Name or team  
Related product: Product or system name  
Related technologies: Technology list  
Status: Draft | Reviewed | Approved | Historical

## Question

What question is this research trying to answer?

## Findings

Summarize the findings.

## Sources

List sources, documents, links, experiments, or interviews.

## Recommendation

Explain what the company should do next.

## Follow-Up

List open questions, risks, and next actions.
```

## Rules

- Separate facts, analysis, assumptions, and recommendations.
- Keep source references where possible.
- Do not present research as product commitment unless approved.
