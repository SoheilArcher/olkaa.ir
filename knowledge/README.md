## Entry Template

Use this structure for new knowledge entries:

```markdown
# Title

Date: YYYY-MM-DD  
Author: Name or team  
Related product: Product or system name  
Related technologies: Technology list  
Status: Draft | Reviewed | Approved | Historical

## Context

Describe the situation, requirement, incident, decision, or problem.

## Decision Or Solution

Describe what was decided, implemented, configured, or fixed.

## Procedure

Document repeatable steps, commands, configuration, screenshots, or diagrams where useful.

## Verification

Explain how the result was tested, monitored, or validated.

## Lessons Learned

Record risks, tradeoffs, future improvements, and what should be remembered.
```
