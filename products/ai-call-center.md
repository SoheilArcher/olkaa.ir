# AI Call Center

## Overview

AI Call Center is a planned AI-assisted customer communication product for call workflows, support routing, summaries, knowledge retrieval, and human handoff.

## Business Problem

Call centers and support teams often handle repeated questions, fragmented customer histories, inconsistent summaries, and slow escalation. Without structured workflows and knowledge access, service quality can vary and managers may lack visibility.

## Solution

AI Call Center will explore and define AI-assisted workflows that support agents, summarize conversations, retrieve approved knowledge, classify requests, and route issues while keeping humans responsible for sensitive decisions and final customer handling.
