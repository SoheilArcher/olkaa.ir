# VPN Accounting

## Overview

VPN Accounting is a planned product line for billing, subscription management, usage tracking, customer records, and access-control coordination for VPN and connectivity services.

## Business Problem

VPN and connectivity services require accurate customer records, subscription status, access control, renewal tracking, billing, and support history. Manual handling can create billing errors, expired access, weak reporting, and customer-service delays.

## Solution

VPN Accounting will provide a structured operational and financial layer for managing VPN customers, plans, subscriptions, renewals, payments, and service access. The product should integrate with network systems only after security and operational controls are defined.

- Implemented: Product concept recorded in the Enterprise Manual.
- In progress: Requirements definition for billing, subscription, and access-control workflows.
- Planned: Customer management, plans, subscriptions, invoices, payments, renewal reminders, access status, and reporting.

- Audit logs for subscription, payment, and access changes.

## Competitive Landscape

VPN Accounting competes with generic billing systems, ISP management platforms, manual spreadsheets, and custom scripts. Differentiation should come from simpler operations, local accounting needs, service-access coordination, and integration with Fava Imen Olka network services.
