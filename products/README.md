# Products

This section is the official enterprise product catalog for Fava Imen Olka.

## Product Catalog

- [Airport ERP](airport-erp.md): Enterprise resource planning platform for airport operations, passenger services, finance, reporting, and operational coordination.
- [Market AI](market-ai.md): Research-stage AI product line for market analysis, data interpretation, signals, and decision-support workflows.
- [VPN Accounting](vpn-accounting.md): Planned billing, subscription, access-control, and accounting platform for VPN and connectivity services.
- [Mail Server](mail-server.md): Planned managed mail server product for organizational email, administration, security, backup, and operational monitoring.
- [NetLog](netlog.md): Planned network logging and audit platform for network activity, retention, reporting, and security visibility.
- [URL Reporting](url-reporting.md): Planned URL monitoring and reporting platform for web access visibility, categorization, audit, and compliance workflows.
- [Active Directory](active-directory.md): Planned identity and directory-service product line for centralized users, groups, policies, access, and audit controls.
- [AI Call Center](ai-call-center.md): Planned AI-assisted call center platform for customer support, call workflows, summaries, routing, and human handoff.

## Product Status Rules

Use one of these product statuses:

- Production
- In Development
- Research
- Planned

Every product document must distinguish implemented, in-progress, and planned capabilities. Do not describe a capability as implemented unless it is delivered, verified, and approved for reference.
