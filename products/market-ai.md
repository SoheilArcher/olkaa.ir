# Market AI

## Overview

Market AI is a research-stage product line for market analysis, data interpretation, decision support, and AI-assisted reporting. It should remain clearly marked as research until product scope, data sources, evaluation methods, and compliance boundaries are approved.

## Business Problem

Business teams often need to interpret market signals, compare opportunities, monitor trends, and convert large volumes of data into actionable decisions. Without structured data pipelines and evaluation rules, analysis may become inconsistent, manual, slow, or difficult to audit.

## Solution

Market AI will explore AI-assisted workflows for collecting, structuring, analyzing, and explaining market information. The product must separate research findings from production claims and should document all data sources, assumptions, limitations, and model behavior.

## Key Features

- Implemented: Product concept recorded in the Enterprise Manual.
- In progress: Research scope, use cases, and data requirements.
- Planned: Market dashboards, signal summaries, trend analysis, report generation, alerts, and explainable decision-support workflows.

## Modules

- Market data intake.
- Data cleaning and normalization.
- Signal and trend analysis.
- Report generation.
- Watchlists and alerts.
- Research notes and source tracking.
- Evaluation and quality review.

## Target Customers

- Internal strategy and product teams.
- Business development teams.
- Analysts and managers.
- Future customers needing market-monitoring and decision-support tools.

## Technology Stack

- To be finalized after research.
- Expected areas: Python, data pipelines, relational or analytical storage, AI models, evaluation scripts, dashboards, and API services.
- Model selection and data handling must follow AI and security standards.

## Deployment

- Research environment first.
- Production deployment must not proceed until data governance, evaluation, monitoring, and user-access requirements are approved.

## Integrations

- Planned: market data sources, internal knowledge base, reporting dashboards, notification channels, and export formats.
- Integrations must identify source reliability, licensing constraints, and update frequency.

## Security

- Source-data governance.
- Access control for sensitive analysis.
- Audit trail for generated reports.
- Clear handling of assumptions, limitations, and model outputs.
- No use of confidential data without approval.

## Product Status

Research

## Roadmap

- Define initial research questions and target users.
- Identify permitted data sources.
- Create evaluation criteria for accuracy, usefulness, and risk.
- Prototype analysis workflows.
- Decide whether to move to planned or in-development status.

## Competitive Landscape

Market AI sits near BI tools, market intelligence platforms, trading analytics tools, and AI research assistants. Differentiation should come from localized workflows, transparent assumptions, domain-specific analysis, and integration with the company's organizational knowledge.

## Future AI Features

- AI-generated market briefs.
- Trend explanation and scenario comparison.
- Natural-language querying over approved datasets.
- Risk and opportunity scoring.
- Automated research summaries with source references.
