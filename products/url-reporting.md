# URL Reporting

## Overview

URL Reporting is a planned reporting product for visibility into web access, URL activity, categorization, compliance review, and operational analysis.

## Business Problem

Organizations may need to understand web access patterns, investigate incidents, support policy enforcement, and produce compliance reports. Without structured URL reporting, reviews become manual, incomplete, and difficult to audit.

## Solution

URL Reporting will provide a controlled reporting layer for URL events, categories, users or groups where permitted, time ranges, dashboards, and audit exports. Privacy, legal, and access-control requirements must be defined before production use.

## Key Features

- Implemented: Product concept recorded in the Enterprise Manual.
- In progress: Definition of reporting scope, roles, and dashboard requirements.
- Planned: URL event collection, categorization, filters, dashboards, scheduled reports, exports, and access-controlled review.

## Modules

- URL event intake.
- Categorization and tagging.
- User, group, or network-source mapping where approved.
- Search and filters.
- Dashboards.
- Scheduled reports.
- Export and audit review.
- Policy and access management.
