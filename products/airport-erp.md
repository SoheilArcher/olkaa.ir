# Airport ERP

## Overview

Airport ERP is the enterprise platform direction for airport operations and aviation-related service management at Fava Imen Olka. It is intended to organize operational workflows, passenger-service processes, finance, reporting, and airport-specific modules in one governed system.

## Business Problem

Airport operations involve many connected workflows: passenger services, CIP/VIP handling, reservation data, check-in coordination, revenue management, display systems, reporting, finance, and operational support. When these workflows are split across separate tools, teams face duplicated data, weak auditability, slow reporting, and inconsistent service quality.

## Solution

Airport ERP will provide a centralized operating system for airport and aviation-service workflows. The product should reuse Fava Imen Olka's documented historical capability in airport systems, FIDS, RMS, check-in software, and CIP/VIP passenger workflows while clearly separating historical experience from new product implementation.
