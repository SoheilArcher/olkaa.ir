# Yearly Executive Report

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Report architecture

The Yearly Executive Report summarizes annual company performance, maturity, risk, and strategy.

## Purpose

- Review company performance across the year.
- Measure product family progress.
- Review annual revenue, recurring revenue, cash flow, and financial stability.
- Summarize major decisions, ADRs, incidents, releases, risks, and lessons learned.
- Set strategic priorities for the next year.

## Report Sections

1. Annual Executive Summary
2. Company Health Review
3. Annual Revenue Review
4. MRR and ARR Review
5. Cash Flow Review
6. Product Family Review
7. Project Delivery Review
8. Customer Growth and Retention
9. Support Quality
10. Infrastructure Reliability
11. Security Posture
12. AI Governance and Usage
13. Documentation Maturity
14. Technical Debt Review
15. Risk Review
16. Research Review
17. Release Review
18. Major Decisions
19. Major ADRs
20. Lessons Learned
21. Strategic Roadmap for Next Year

## Annual Measures

The yearly report should include:

- Growth indicators.
- Stability indicators.
- Product maturity indicators.
- Customer relationship indicators.
- Operational reliability indicators.
- Documentation and knowledge maturity indicators.
- Risk trend indicators.

## Output

The yearly report should be treated as the annual executive record for Fava Imen Olka.

## Strategic Use

The yearly report should feed:

- Next-year roadmap.
- Product investment decisions.
- Hiring and team planning.
- Infrastructure investment.
- Security planning.
- Documentation and knowledge governance.
