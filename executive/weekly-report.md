# Weekly Executive Report

Version: v1.0.0  
Status: Report architecture

The Weekly Executive Report gives the CEO a management review of company execution for the week.

## Purpose

- Review progress against weekly goals.
- Identify blocked projects and high-impact risks.
- Measure product, customer, documentation, and support health.
- Prepare decisions for leadership review.

## Report Sections

1. Weekly Executive Summary
2. Company Health Trend
3. Revenue and Cash Review
4. Product Portfolio Status
5. Project Delivery Status
6. Active Customers and Relationship Risks
7. Support Tickets and SLA Review
8. Infrastructure and Security Review
9. AI Usage and AI Risk Review
10. Roadmap Progress
11. Documentation Coverage
12. Technical Debt
13. Recent Decisions
14. Recent ADRs
15. Open Risks
16. Next Week Priorities

## Required Inputs

- Weekly finance summary.
- Product owner updates.
- Project owner updates.
- Customer account updates.
- Support ticket metrics.
- Monitoring incidents.
- Security alerts.
- Documentation review.
- Roadmap changes.
- ADR and decision logs.

## Review Workflow

1. System prepares the weekly report.
2. Module owners review their sections.
3. CEO reviews company-level summary.
4. Decisions and actions are assigned owners.
5. Open risks are updated.
6. Weekly report is archived as company memory.

## Output

The report should create an immutable weekly snapshot after approval.
